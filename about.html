Our Story

Made in Pakistan. Trusted Worldwide.

Life Line Gloves was founded with a singular purpose: to manufacture the highest quality protective equipment that genuinely protects the people who need it most. Based in Korangi, Karachi — Pakistan's industrial heartland — we began as a specialist leather glove manufacturer and have grown into a comprehensive PPE solutions provider serving clients across the globe.

Over more than 15 years, we have built a reputation for consistent quality, reliable delivery and responsive customer service that sets us apart. Our facility employs skilled craftsmen and modern production equipment to ensure every product meets or exceeds international safety standards.

From a small leather workshop to a full-scale PPE manufacturer exporting to over 30 countries — our journey reflects our commitment to excellence and our belief that worker safety should never be compromised.
