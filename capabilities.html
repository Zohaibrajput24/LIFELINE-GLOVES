Manufacturing Capabilities

End-to-end manufacturing solutions tailored to your business needs.

What We Offer

Your Vision.
Our Expertise.

Life Line Gloves is not just a manufacturer — we are your manufacturing partner. From concept to delivery, we handle every aspect of production so you can focus on growing your brand and business.

Whether you need a single SKU with your label or a complete custom-engineered product line, our experienced team has the infrastructure, expertise, and commitment to deliver.

Core Capabilities

How We Manufacture For You

01

OEM Manufacturing

We manufacture to your exact specifications. Provide us with your technical drawings, material requirements, and standards — and we'll produce products under your brand name at competitive prices.

  • Custom specifications & designs
  • Minimum order quantities from 500 pairs
  • International certifications available
  • Consistent quality across all batches
02

ODM Manufacturing

Share your concept or market requirement and our in-house design team will develop the product from scratch. We handle design, prototyping, testing, and full production.

  • In-house product design team
  • Rapid prototyping within 2–3 weeks
  • Market-ready product development
  • Confidential agreements available
03

Private Label Solutions

Launch your own PPE brand without building a factory. We produce ready-made gloves, workwear, and footwear with your logo, labels, and custom packaging.

  • Custom logo embossing & embroidery
  • Branded hang tags & labels
  • Custom box & polybag packaging
  • Low MOQ for branded products
04

Quality Control & Assurance

Every production batch undergoes rigorous in-house quality checks at every stage — from raw material inspection to pre-shipment final inspection.

  • In-line production QC checks
  • Pre-shipment inspection (PSI)
  • Third-party lab testing available
  • Compliance with EN, ANSI, ISO standards
05

Raw Material Sourcing

We maintain strong relationships with premium leather tanneries, fabric mills, and chemical suppliers to ensure consistent material quality across all production runs.

  • Premium leather from certified tanneries
  • FR & hi-vis certified fabrics
  • Chemical-tested dyes & coatings
  • Sustainable sourcing options
06

Export Packaging & Logistics

We prepare all orders export-ready — proper packaging, documentation, and coordination with freight forwarders for seamless international shipping.

  • Export-compliant packing
  • Full shipping documentation
  • FCL & LCL shipment support
  • Delivery to any global port

Our Process

From Inquiry to Delivery

A simple, transparent manufacturing process designed to keep you informed at every step.

1

Inquiry & Consultation

Share your requirements — product type, quantity, specifications, certifications needed. We respond within 24 hours.

2

Quotation & Agreement

We prepare a detailed quotation including pricing, lead time, payment terms and MOQ. NDA available on request.

3

Sample Development

We produce pre-production samples for your approval. Revisions are incorporated until you are 100% satisfied.

4

Production & QC

Full production with in-line QC checkpoints. Updates and progress reports shared throughout the manufacturing run.

5

Inspection & Shipment

Pre-shipment inspection, export packing, full documentation, and on-time delivery to your destination.
