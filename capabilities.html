Core Capabilities

How We Manufacture For You

Six pillars of manufacturing excellence that make Life Line Gloves your most reliable PPE partner.

01

OEM Manufacturing

We manufacture to your exact specifications — technical drawings, materials and standards — under your brand name at competitive prices.

  • Custom specs & designs
  • MOQ from 500 pairs
  • International certifications
  • Consistent batch quality
02

ODM Manufacturing

Share your concept and our in-house design team develops the product from scratch — design, prototyping, testing and full production.

  • In-house design team
  • Rapid prototyping 2–3 weeks
  • Market-ready development
  • NDA available on request
03

Private Label Solutions

Launch your own PPE brand without building a factory. We produce with your logo, labels and custom packaging ready to ship.

  • Custom logo embossing
  • Branded hang tags & labels
  • Custom box & polybag packing
  • Low MOQ for branded products
04

Quality Control

Rigorous in-house QC from raw material inspection through to pre-shipment final inspection on every single batch.

  • In-line production checks
  • Pre-shipment inspection (PSI)
  • Third-party lab testing
  • EN, ANSI, ISO compliance
05

Raw Material Sourcing

Strong partnerships with certified leather tanneries, fabric mills and chemical suppliers ensure consistent material quality.

  • Certified leather tanneries
  • FR & hi-vis certified fabrics
  • Chemical-tested dyes & coatings
  • Sustainable sourcing options
06

Export & Logistics

Export-ready packaging, full shipping documentation and freight coordination for seamless delivery to any global port.

  • Export-compliant packing
  • Full shipping documentation
  • FCL & LCL shipment support
  • Delivery to any global port
