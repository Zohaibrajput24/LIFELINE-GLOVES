About Life Line Gloves

Made in Pakistan. Trusted Worldwide.

Founded in Korangi, Karachi — Pakistan's industrial heartland — Life Line Gloves began as a specialist leather glove maker and grew into a comprehensive PPE solutions provider exporting to over 30 countries worldwide.

Our facility combines skilled craftsmen with modern production equipment, ensuring every product meets or exceeds international safety standards — delivered on time, every time.
